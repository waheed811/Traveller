An example particularly well-fitting to represent the current status of civilization is the one of the frog in the pot. If we put a live frog into a pot full of boiling water, the frog will suddenly jump outside, saving its life.

But if we put the frog into a pot full of cold water, and we light the fire under the pot, the frog will stay inside, and will slowly die. Our civilization is like that frog, and the pot we are in is getting more and more dangerous.
